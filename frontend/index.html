How ThreatLens Works

Four independent detection layers fused into a single weighted verdict

01
🧠

LSTM Neural Net

A bidirectional LSTM model trained on thousands of phishing and legitimate emails detects subtle language manipulation patterns invisible to rule-based systems.

02
📊

TF-IDF Classifier

Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency vectorization maps the email against a phishing vocabulary corpus, catching keyword-based attacks and pattern clusters.

03
⚙️

Rule Engine

Expert heuristics flag urgency language, impersonation signals (CEO, HR, Finance), action-link proximity, trusted domain calibration, and generic greetings.

04
🔗

URL Reputation

Extracts all links from the email body and scores each against known shorteners, suspicious TLDs, missing HTTPS, embedded @-signs, and SharePoint auth tricks.
